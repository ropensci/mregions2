# mregions2: Access Data from Marineregions.org: Gazetteer & Data Products

Explore and retrieve marine geospatial data from the Marine Regions
Gazetteer <https://marineregions.org/gazetteer.php?p=webservices> and
the Marine Regions Data Products
<https://marineregions.org/webservices.php>.
